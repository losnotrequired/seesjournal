By Neighborhood

where to find art across the county

Balboa Park BP

  • San Diego Museum of Art — European, American, Asian and contemporary collections.
  • MOPA@SDMA — the Museum of Photographic Arts.
  • Mingei International Museum — folk art, craft and design.
  • Timken Museum of Art — European old masters. Always free.
  • ICA San Diego / Central — contemporary exhibitions and NextGen.
  • Spanish Village Art Center — 200+ working artist studios.
  • Centro Cultural de la Raza — Chicano, Mexican and Indigenous art.

La Jolla LJ

  • MCASD — the Museum of Contemporary Art San Diego.
  • Quint Gallery — contemporary art since 1981, on Girard Ave.
  • Tasende Gallery — modern and contemporary sculpture, on Prospect.
  • Madison Gallery — contemporary painting and sculpture.
  • R.B. Stevenson Gallery — modern and contemporary works.
  • L&G Projects — emerging and mid-career contemporary artists.
  • Joseph Bellows Gallery — fine art photography.
  • Contemporary Fine Arts — original paintings and sculpture.
  • Athenaeum Music & Arts Library — exhibitions and concerts.
  • … plus more along “Gallery Row” on Prospect St and Girard Ave.

Barrio Logan BL

  • Chicano Park — the nation’s largest outdoor mural collection, under the Coronado Bridge.
  • Bread & Salt — galleries and artist studios in a former bakery.
  • Best Practice — artist-run contemporary project space.
  • Athenaeum Art Center — exhibitions and printmaking studios.
  • La Bodega Gallery — community gallery and events.

North Park & University Heights NP

  • Ray Street Arts District — 30+ galleries and studios; home of the monthly Ray at Night walk.
  • Art on 30th — art center with working studios and juried shows (Ashton Gallery).
  • The Studio Door — gallery and artist studios on the 30th St corridor.

Liberty Station LS

  • ARTS DISTRICT Liberty Station — dozens of galleries and studios in the former Naval barracks.
  • Visions Art Museum — contemporary quilts and textile art.
  • San Diego Watercolor Society — monthly member exhibitions.

Downtown DT

  • Sparks Gallery, Gaslamp — regional contemporary art.
  • The New Children’s Museum — immersive, art-driven installations.
  • San Diego Central Library — gallery shows plus 150+ works from the city collection.

North County OC · ES

  • Oceanside Museum of Art — Southern California artists.
  • California Center for the Arts, Escondido — museum and performances.
  • ICA San Diego / North, Encinitas — contemporary exhibitions (formerly Lux).
  • Cannon Art Gallery, Carlsbad — exhibitions at the Dove Library.
  • Distinction Gallery, Escondido — contemporary art and artist studios.
  • Front Porch Gallery, Carlsbad — community-focused shows.
  • Hill Street Country Club, Oceanside — alternative art space and gallery.

Tips & Notes

before you go

This Week

  • Busiest day: Saturday, May 2 — 2 things on.

Before You Go

  • Often free: the Timken is always free, and commercial galleries (Quint, Sparks, Madison, R.B. Stevenson, Tasende, Joseph Bellows) are free to visit. Several museums have monthly free days — confirm on each venue’s site.
  • Balboa Park: parking is free but lots fill on weekends and during big events — arrive early or use the tram.
  • Closed days: many museums close Monday and/or Tuesday — check hours before heading out.
  • Art walks: most run monthly on a set night — Barrio Art Crawl and Ray at Night on the 2nd Saturday, La Jolla and Oceanside on the First Friday. Confirm the lineup on the organizer’s page.
  • Always verify: small galleries and DIY spaces keep limited or by-appointment hours — call or check socials before traveling.
