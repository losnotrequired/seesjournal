About

On View is an independent listing of art across San Diego County — museum shows, gallery openings, artist talks, and the monthly art walks that knit the scene together. It is built for people who want one clear place to see what is happening this week and plan a route around it.

The guide is not affiliated with any museum, gallery, or venue. Listings are gathered from public calendars and venue announcements and organized by date and neighborhood. Dates, times, and lineups change — always confirm with the venue before you travel, especially for receptions and art walks.
